How to Take Stunning Pictures Using a Magnetic Selfie Display

Learn how to take professional-quality photos using a magnetic selfie display. Perfect for creators who want better lighting, stable shots, and wireless control.

Step 1: Set Up Your Display

Attach your phone magnetically and ensure a secure fit. Clean your lens before shooting.

Step 2: Use Good Lighting

Use natural or balanced lighting. Avoid harsh backlight.

Step 3: Preview and Frame Your Shot

Use mirroring and feedback to compose the shot before capturing.

Step 4: Take Multiple Shots & Edit

Take several angles and edit for brightness and contrast.
